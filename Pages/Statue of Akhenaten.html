Statue of Akhenaten

Status

The status of Akhenaten’s mummy is highly complex because its identity remained a subject of intense debate for decades, only to be officially confirmed recently through DNA analysis.

Great Achievements

The mummy now widely accepted to be Akhenaten was previously known simply as the "Mummy from Tomb 55" (KV55). General Condition: The mummy is poorly preserved compared to other royal mummies and suffered significant damage in antiquity. Finding Context: The mummy was found nude and incompletely embalmed, covered only in remnants of gold embellishments. It was placed in a repurposed coffin whose original owner's images had been vandalized

Age at Death

Skeletal studies suggest he died around 25 to 35 years old
